Welcome to Belgrade

Belgrade (/ˈbɛlɡreɪd/ BEL-grayd; Serbian: Beograd / Београд, lit.
'White City', pronounced [beǒɡrad] (About this soundlisten);
names in other languages)
is the capital and largest city of Serbia. It is located at the confluence of the Sava and Danube rivers
and the crossroads of the Pannonian Plain and the Balkan Peninsula.
